Research Opportunities

We are currently seeking a qualified candidate for a PhD scholarship in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at North Carolina A&T University. The scholarship is immediately available and focuses on the design of secure, explainable, and/or privacy-preserving protocols for autonomous systems, vehicular ad hoc networks, and smart grid.

Applicants should have a strong background in one or more of the following areas: applied cryptography, blockchain, machine learning (specifically deep neural networks, adversarial AI, explainable AI), and privacy-preserving machine learning.

Requirements include:

  1. Master's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Applied Math or a related discipline.

  2. Basic knowledge of applied cryptography.

  3. Strong knowledge of deep machine learning, including deep feed-forward neural networks, deep neural networks (LSTM, GRU, CNN).

  4. Excellent English writing and verbal communication skills.

  5. Proficient programming skills in Python (PyTorch, Keras, Scikit-learn, etc.) and MATLAB.

  6. High TOEFL and GRE scores.

Interested students please send the following documents as a single PDF file to mnmahmoudATncatDOTedu

Application:

  1. Curriculum Vitae (CV), including a summary of educational background, grades, and a list of publications.

  2. A short research statement or cover letter (maximum 1 page) outlining your research interests and goals.

  3. Names and email addresses of at least three references.

  4. A soft copy of one or two recent publications that you consider representative of your research work.
